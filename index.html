Welcome to the ECPL Management System

With Emerald City's growing population, more residents than ever are turning to the **Emerald City Public Library (ECPL)** for their reading needs. Daily patron registrations have doubled, and book circulation has reached record highs.

To support this rapid growth, ECPL is upgrading from an outdated system to a **modern, database-driven platform**. Our system streamlines book management, organizes author records, and efficiently tracks patron borrowing activity. Whether the library continues to expand its collection or welcomes new members, our system is designed to scale seamlessly.

Table Name Description
Books Manages book details such as title, genre, publication year, and author.
Authors Stores author names and birth years.
Patrons Tracks library members, their names, and membership start dates.
Loans Manages book checkouts, linking books to patrons with loan and return dates.
